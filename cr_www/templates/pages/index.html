CR is a new platform for learning about and responding to critical news
{##}

Be Conscious

CR is a simple assistant that helps you understand the news, ask powerful questions, and develop your own, educated perspective on complex current events. You can open CR in Chrome, and soon on your iOS device.

Identify Bias, Have Perspective

On CR, you can contribute to Insights–– crowdsourced polls that help decode a journalist's take on a story. Insights enforce transparency, and help you easily compare media perspectives on complex current events.

Raise critical questions

Breaking news leaves us wanting to know more. On CR, you can Ask and Follow questions that are important to you, and be notified directly by a journalist when she has answered your specific question in a new story.

Interpret the world

Readers, journalists, and news organizations will soon be able to use CR to answer powerful questions about how we interpret the world around us.
